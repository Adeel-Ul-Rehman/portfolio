MY EDUCATION

School

The name of my school is C.D.G. Govt. Boys High School Chota Goun. It was established in 1976 and is located in Lahore city, in Shahdrah Islam Pura. I spent 10 years of my life there and passed my matriculation in 2020. It wasn't a big school, just a small one, but it had great personalities and teachers. I made many friends who brought fun into my life and supported me in every aspect. We were together through all the ups and downs of school life. I have very precious memories and unique experiences from my school days that life has given me. In these pictures, you can see glimpses of my school that gave me a great start.

College

My college is Islamia College Civil Lines Lahore. I got admission there in 2020 and passed my intermediate exams in 2022. It's located in Lahore, and despite being one of the biggest educational institutes in Pakistan, it has maintained a nurturing environment with supportive teachers and friends. I've had countless memorable experiences and learned invaluable lessons during my time there. Whether it was participating in extracurricular activities, studying in the library, or simply hanging out with friends, every moment has contributed to shaping who I am today. Islamia College has not only provided me with academic knowledge but also instilled in me a sense of responsibility and independence that will stay with me throughout my life.

University

I'm studying Computer Science at University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Kala Shah Kaku campus, one of Pakistan's biggest government universities. I began my studies here in 2023 after taking a year off due to a leg injury from an accident. Initially, I had planned to join COMSAT University Lahore for software engineering in January 2023, but had to change plans due to my injury. Despite not starting at UET's main campus, I'm happy with my choice. Here, I've learned more than I ever imagined, thanks to supportive friends who also act as mentors, introducing me to new technologies and techniques. It's been an enriching journey, and I'm eager to continue growing and become a skilled computer scientist by the time I graduate.
